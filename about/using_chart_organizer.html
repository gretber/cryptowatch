Using
The chart organizer

 

[This applies to "desktop mode" users only*]

Hardcore Trading

The chart organizer is how you add new stocks, currencies, and indices. Your organizer shows a list of all the charts you have opened. If you don't like the order of your charts or rather certain charts beside each other, click on their name in the list and rearrange it how you want. You can see your changes in real-time, you won't have to page refresh! Instead of rearranging your charts, you can add to them also. In the add chart box, begin typing to search for a chart to add. You can click on the charts you wish to add or type its name exactly and press enter.

Rearranging charts

Easy, click their names and drag them in place.

Adding charts

Type in the search box, click the charts that you want or type exactly the chart name.
